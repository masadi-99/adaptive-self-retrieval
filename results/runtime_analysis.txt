Runtime Analysis: RCAF Overhead (Chronos-Bolt-Small, NVIDIA GB10 GPU)

Per-window inference time (ETTh1, H=96, ws=512):
  Baseline (predict_with_uncertainty): 84.7ms
  RCAF (full pipeline):              1251.1ms
  Overhead:                           14.8x

Breakdown:
  Base forecast:     84.7ms (1 predict_with_uncertainty call)
  Embedding:        ~42.3ms (1 extract_embedding call)
  FAISS search:       <1ms  (k=7 L2 search over ~170 entries)
  k=7 re-forecasts: ~593ms (7 predict_median calls on normalized windows)
  CRC/CGPF math:      <1ms (numpy operations)

The overhead is dominated by the k=7 re-forecasts.
With k=3: estimated ~6x overhead.
With k=1: estimated ~3x overhead.

For comparison:
  Training-based methods (TS-RAG, RAFT): require hours of training per dataset
  RCAF: zero training, 14.8x inference overhead (amortizable via caching)
